Instructions:

Welcome to photoBook. Here you'll be able to browse images in a nostalgic manner and save them in your very own photoBook!

You can either change page using the arrows in the side or by manually flipping the pages at the corners.

To add an image to your own photoBook, hold down the mouse over an image you like and drag it into the 'My photoBook' album to the right. To view your images, refresh your browswer and then click on the 'My photoBook' album, and you'll be taken to the album.
